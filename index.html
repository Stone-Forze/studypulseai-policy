Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

Welcome to StudyPulse AI. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using StudyPulse AI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

StudyPulse AI is designed to be a privacy-first tool. We do not require you to create an account to use the basic features.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect personal identifiers like your name, address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide them via support emails.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-identifiable information such as device model, OS version, and app usage statistics to improve performance.
  • Input Content: Text or data you paste into the AI tools is processed in real-time. We do not store your study materials or personal notes on our own servers.

2. Third-Party AI Service Providers

To provide advanced AI functionalities, StudyPulse AI routes your requests through various third-party providers. These providers receive the text you input to generate responses. We currently use:

  • Google Gemini
  • OpenRouter
  • Groq & Cerebras Cloud
  • Mistral AI & Cohere
  • SambaNova & Together AI
  • Hugging Face & GitHub Models

Each of these providers has its own privacy policy regarding data handling. We recommend reviewing their respective policies.

3. Advertising (Ads Policy)

StudyPulse AI uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. To show relevant ads, AdMob may collect and use:

  • Advertising ID: To provide personalized ads and frequency capping.
  • Device Information: To ensure ads are formatted correctly for your screen.

You can opt-out of personalized advertising through your Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

4. Simple vs. Premium Features

  • Simple (Free) Version: Supported by advertisements. Basic tool access is provided with standard API rotation.
  • Premium Version (If Applicable): If you upgrade to Premium, we may use your Google Play Billing information to manage your subscription. Premium users may enjoy an ad-free experience and priority access to faster AI models.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

StudyPulse AI is intended for students aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
